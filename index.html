Radar chart

This is a representation of the keys in which the songs are written, keeping in mind the mode used to play them.
The red area indicates the mode major, meanwhile the blue one the mode minor.

In this chart, we can observe some statistics on the duration and the loudness of the songs.

Donutpie chart

Here is shown the correlation between the danceability of the songs and the features of tempo and time signature. So, for the three types of danceability, are shown the distributions of the rhythmic features.

Grouped Bar Chart

This is an analysis of the values of energy and valence of the songs.
The blue bars indicate the occurences for the energy and the orange ones those for the valence.

Bubble Chart

In every bubble are shown the occurences of the values for the features of acousticness, instrumentalness and speechiness.
